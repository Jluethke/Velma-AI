## Phase 1: ASSESS Output

**BMR Calculation (Mifflin-St Jeor):**
- Weight: 185 lbs = 83.9 kg
- Height: 70 in = 177.8 cm
- Age: 30
- BMR = (10 x 83.9) + (6.25 x 177.8) - (5 x 30) + 5
- BMR = 839 + 1111.25 - 150 + 5 = **1805 cal/day**

**TDEE:**
- Activity multiplier: 1.55 (moderately active)
- TDEE = 1805 x 1.55 = **2798 cal/day**

**Cut target:**
- Deficit: 500 cal/day (target ~1 lb/week loss)
- Daily target: 2798 - 500 = **2300 cal/day**
- Safe? 2300 > 1500 minimum. Yes.
