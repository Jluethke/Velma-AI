# SEO Cluster Builder -- CRM Software Topic Cluster

## Scenario

A CRM software company wants to build topical authority around "CRM" keywords. They currently rank for their brand name only. They have 12 existing blog posts with no cluster strategy. Goal: rank for high-intent keywords within 6 months.

## Phase 1: SEED -- Keyword Analysis

**Seed Keywords Provided:** "CRM software", "CRM for small business", "customer relationship management"

**Search Intent Classification:**

| CRM software | 40,500/mo | Commercial investigation | Mid-funnel |
| what is CRM | 22,000/mo | Informational | Top-funnel |

| CRM free | 12,100/mo | Transactional | Bottom-funnel |
