## What IS Covered

1. **Production-backed currency minting**: Issuance proportional to verified multi-resource physical production with configurable per-category weights
2. **Multi-stage production verification**: Bounds checking, statistical anomaly detection, hash integrity, multi-signature auditor approval
3. **Hash-chain audit ledger**: Append-only tamper-evident transaction recording with cryptographic chain linking
4. **Algorithmic monetary policy**: Taylor-rule variant targeting configurable production-relative inflation, four policy phases, supply adjustment
5. **Money velocity tracking**: Velocity-informed supply decisions
6. **Reserve management**: Configurable reserve ratio bounds with prioritized replenishment
7. **Federation governance**: Multi-stage pipeline (observe, assess, check, modulate, enforce) with trust-derived authority levels
8. **Partition detection and recovery**: Freeze conditions based on instance response rates, recovery boost for trust healing
9. **UBI distribution**: Phase-scaled periodic distribution to participating zones
10. **Inter-zone settlement**: Currency transfers linked to physical resource trades via reference identifiers
